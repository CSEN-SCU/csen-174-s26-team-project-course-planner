Turn your transcript into clearer next-term choices.

SCU Course Planner is an LLM-powered web app for SCU undergraduates who need help with course planning. The product vision is to combine your transcript and course ratings into stronger suggestions; this prototype focuses on transcript input plus SCU catalog context to deliver explainable next-term options—cutting through scattered information and Workday friction without replacing your advisor when you need a human.

Who It’s For

Especially upperclassmen with more electives, transfer and international students, and anyone working long hours—anyone who needs faster, more confident planning during enrollment and quarter build-out.

What It Solves

The pain of suboptimal picks, stress, and extra advising cycles when planning info is fragmented and “good choice” is hard to see—especially when workload and fit should vary by course type. Unlike relying only on Workday, spreadsheets, or advisor availability, this tool automates a first pass so you get clearer, explainable next-term options faster.

How To Use

Upload a JPG, PDF, or TXT of your transcript (or paste the text) in the Input page, then generate a top-three course recommendation with short reasons and a brief summary you can save for later in the Results page. You can then look back at your results in the History page or use them to generate a long term plan in the Four Year Plan page.
