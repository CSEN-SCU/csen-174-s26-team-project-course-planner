A transcript-driven course planning prototype for SCU undergrads

This prototype helps you quickly answer: what you can take this term and what to prioritize, even when information is scattered across Workday and multiple pages. Provide your major (and academic progress export), and the app generates a candidate pool, ranked recommendations, and clear rationales for a class demo.

Open the app How to read results

Who it’s for

  • Students who want to plan a term faster
  • Anyone comparing teaching quality vs workload
  • Transfer/international/working students with limited time

What it solves

  • Turns your completed courses into prereq signals
  • Builds a filterable candidate pool for the term
  • Produces ranked recommendations with risks and alternatives

How to use

  1. Open the app
  2. Paste a public major requirements link
  3. Generate: review missing requirements and the ranked list

What you’ll see

The app reads your Academic Progress export and a public requirements page. It then lists what’s still missing and recommends eligible course sections for the selected term. If the backend is configured with GEMINI_API_KEY (or OPENAI_API_KEY), it can also generate nicer explanations.
