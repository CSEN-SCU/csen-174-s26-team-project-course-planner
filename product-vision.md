The SCU Course Planner is an LLM-powered web app that automates schedule building for SCU undergrads by analyzing past transcripts and course ratings to recommend the optimal path forward. It eliminates the frustration of navigating Workday or waiting for an academic advisor by giving you instant, personalized course planning.

## How might we…

**How might we** help students turn **academic progress**, **priorities (difficulty vs. quality)**, and **course type** (e.g., core requirement vs. major-interest) into **clearer, explainable next-term choices** **as we aim to** reduce **stress, advising back-and-forth, and low confidence** during quarter planning?

## Key insights (Problem Framing Canvas)

- **Separate signals:** Treat teaching quality and difficulty/workload as different dimensions where possible; a single “rating score” can mislead decisions.
- **Context-dependent priorities:** What “good” means should vary by course type (e.g., core vs. major-interest), not one global rule.
- **Who feels this most:** Upperclassmen at quarter planning, plus transfer, working, and international students with less time or familiarity with offerings and Workday.
- **Accessibility:** Plan for inclusive UX (e.g., screen reader–compatible patterns) as the product matures.
